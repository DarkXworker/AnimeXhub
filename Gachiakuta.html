Rudo, ek teenager tribesfolk, floating city “Sphere” ke slums mein rehta hai. Jab uske foster‑father Regto ki murder ke liye use framed kar diya jaata hai, use “The Pit”—ek dystopian trash‑filled abyss—mein exile kiya jaata hai. Wahan, trash beasts se bachi jaati hai, aur Enjin ke Cleaners team se milkar uski “Giver” power jagti hai—objects mein life bharne ki unique ability. Ab Rudo revenge aur justice ki journey shuru karta hai, Cleaners ki fight mein symbolic aur visceral battle unleash karte hue. Studio Bones ne gritty visuals, graffiti art aur rap‑metal soundtrack ke saath intense world‑building create kiya hai.
